How I Started My Editing Journey ?¿

My editing journey began when I was in class eight, during the time when TikTok was trending. I used to watch various editing content and wonder how they were created. Curious to learn, I turned to YouTube, where many creators recommended PowerDirector—which became my first editing software. CapCut existed back then but wasn’t popular yet. So, I experimented on my own, using myself as the model and shooting videos to practice editing. Over time, my skills improved, leading me to where I am today.

✨ Fun fact: My fascination with editing started because I wanted to add a flying helicopter to my video—and that small curiosity sparked my entire journey!
