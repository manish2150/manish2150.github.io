How I Started Editing

My journey as a video editor began as a curious teenager, spending hours on TikTok and watching editing tutorials, dreaming of creating videos that truly stand out. One day, I saw a video with a helicopter effect and was determined to try it myself back when CapCut wasnt popular, and the top tools were KineMaster and PowerDirector. PowerDirector became my first editing software, and from that moment, my hobby transformed into a deep passion for visual storytelling.



Over the years, Ive honed my skills through countless projects, experimenting with different styles, techniques, and software to deliver cinematic visuals that captivate audiences. Today, I work as a video editor in Pokhara, bringing creativity, precision, and storytelling to every project I take on, whether its short films, ads, or promotional reels.
